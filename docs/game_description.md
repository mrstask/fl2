# Ashfall: Wastes of Orion - Game Description

## 1. Core Vision
- Genre: isometric, turn-based post-apocalyptic RPG
- Inspiration: Fallout 2 style structure and player freedom
- Perspective: 2D/2.5D isometric camera
- Tone: dark humor, moral ambiguity, survival and politics

## 2. Core Fantasy
The player is a survivor sent from an isolated settlement into a hostile wasteland to solve a home crisis.  
Player identity is fully shaped by choices: diplomat, mercenary, scientist, thief, faction agent, or ruthless warlord.

## 3. Design Pillars
1. Choice and consequence: quests have multiple valid solutions with delayed outcomes.
2. Deep RPG systems: stat/skill/trait/perk builds with meaningful tradeoffs.
3. Tactical turn-based combat: AP economy, targeted attacks, weapon role diversity.
4. Reactive world simulation: faction reputation, companion loyalty, evolving settlements.

## 4. Core Gameplay Loop
1. Explore global map and handcrafted locations.
2. Interact via dialogue, stealth, trade, investigation, hacking, or combat.
3. Earn XP, loot, resources, and faction standing.
4. Upgrade build and equipment to access harder content.
5. Resolve major conflicts that branch from previous decisions.

## 5. World Setup
Retro-futuristic wasteland formed after corporate wars and ecological collapse.

### Major Factions
- The Foundry Union (industry and enforced order)
- Verdant Creed (bio-engineered faith and control)
- Free Caravans (trade federation and neutrality)
- Night Circuit (raiders and old-tech scavengers)

### Main Story Arc
Save the home settlement from collapse while uncovering a conspiracy around pre-war reactor technology.

### Ending Structure
Multiple macro endings and localized epilogues driven by faction outcomes, companion fates, and morality.

## 6. RPG Systems (High-Level)
- Attributes: Strength, Perception, Endurance, Charisma, Intelligence, Agility, Luck
- Skills: Guns, Melee, Medicine, Science, Speech, Lockpick, Sneak, Barter, Repair, Survival
- Traits: powerful bonuses with strong drawbacks
- Perks: periodic, build-defining progression unlocks
- Reputation: tracked per faction and settlement
- Companions: recruitable NPCs with quests, loyalty, and tactical roles

## 7. Combat
- Turn-based encounters with Action Points (AP)
- Attack modes: standard, aimed, burst, melee specials
- Spatial tactics: line-of-sight, range falloff, cover interactions
- Status effects: bleeding, stun, radiation, poison, crippled limbs
- Enemy families: raiders, mutants, robots, wildlife, faction squads

## 8. Scope Targets
### Vertical Slice
- 1 town hub
- 1 dungeon/location
- 3-5 branching quests
- 1 companion
- 6-8 enemy types
- Basic character creation, progression, and turn-based combat

### Full Game
- 8-12 major locations
- 40+ quests
- 4-6 companions
- 3-5 primary endings plus epilogues

## 9. Success Criteria
- At least 3 viable playstyles: combat, stealth, social
- Most major quests support non-combat resolution
- Player decisions visibly alter world state later in the game
- High replayability through builds and faction routes
